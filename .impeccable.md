## Design Context

### Users
Vantage Point serves advertisers and media agencies booking out-of-home campaigns, and billboard vendors managing inventory and approvals. Both groups have equal priority. Authentication must help returning users enter the correct role workspace quickly and help new organizations register with confidence.

### Brand Personality
Premium, cinematic, and dramatic. The experience should communicate trust, operational authority, and a sense of entering a high-value media exchange without becoming theatrical or difficult to use.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a cinematic editorial direction: deep tinted surfaces, deliberate contrast, large characterful typography, restrained geometric details, and asymmetric composition. Support both light and cinematic themes while avoiding generic glassmorphism, neon-on-dark styling, repetitive cards, and excessive monospace copy.

### Design Principles
1. Give advertisers and vendors equal visual weight and equally clear paths.
2. Make authentication feel like a confident threshold into a premium exchange.
3. Keep forms direct and readable; drama belongs in composition, typography, and motion.
4. Meet WCAG AA with visible focus, keyboard operation, focus trapping, readable contrast, and reduced-motion support.
5. Use progressive disclosure for demo access and secondary actions so the primary authentication task stays dominant.
