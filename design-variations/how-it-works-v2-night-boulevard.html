HOW IT WORKS

From search to live campaign in six steps.

Deep workflows sit behind each role. Here's the full sequence.

01 Buyer

Search

Filter by city, format, traffic volume, and price range.

02 Buyer

Compare

View specs, daily rates, traffic data, and photos side by side.

03 Buyer

Price

Daily rate × flight duration = total. No fees added at checkout.

04 Buyer

Schedule

Lock calendar dates. A pending booking record is created instantly.

05 Vendor

Approve

Vendor confirms availability and accepts the booking.

06 Both

Report

Campaign goes live. Telemetry tracks delivery status.
